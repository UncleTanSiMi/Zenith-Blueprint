Why This Workshop

YOUR BRAND DESERVES BETTER THAN A PHONE SNAPSHOT

What You'll Learn

THE FULL PRODUCTION PIPELINE

From concept to final cut — you'll build a real AI-generated video during the session.

Module 01
AI Prompting for Video Marketing
Master the fundamentals of prompting AI tools effectively. Learn how to brief AI the same way you'd brief a creative director — with context, tone, and intent — to get outputs that actually reflect your brand's unique selling points.
ChatGPT Gemini Prompt Frameworks
Module 02
Creative Direction & Storyboarding with AI
Use AI to brainstorm video concepts tailored to your brand or product. Generate shot lists, visual moods, and narrative arcs that resonate with your target audience.
Gemini ChatGPT Storyboard Generation
Module 03
AI Image Generation
Generate photorealistic visuals, lifestyle imagery, and ambient scenes that complement your actual photos. Learn staging prompts, lighting cues, and how to maintain visual consistency across a video.
Gemini Pro ChatGPT / DALL·E Image Prompting
Module 04
AI Video Generation with Google Flow & Veo
Turn your storyboard and images into actual video clips using Google's cutting-edge Veo model via Flow. Learn clip duration, motion style, and how to generate multiple versions to get the best output for your project.
Google Flow Veo Video Prompting
Module 05 — Full Day Only
Editing & Final Assembly in CapCut
Stitch your AI-generated clips into a polished, shareable property video. Learn how to add captions, transitions, background music, and subtitles optimised for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and WhatsApp sharing. You'll leave with a completed video ready to post.
CapCut Reels / TikTok Format Caption & Music
ℹ

Half Day option covers Modules 01–04 (prompting, creative direction, image gen, and video gen). Module 05 (CapCut editing) is exclusive to the Full Day programme. Both formats include hands-on practice time.

Before You Register

WHAT YOU NEED

💎
Google Gemini Pro
An active Google Gemini Pro subscription is required — this is needed to access Google Flow for AI video generation. You can sign up for the 1-month free trial before the workshop, or subscribe at approx. S$28/month. Serious participants will find it worth keeping.
💻
Laptop & Stable Internet
Bring a laptop (Mac or Windows) with a modern browser. For F2F participants, WiFi is provided. For online participants, ensure a stable broadband connection — AI video generation is bandwidth intensive.
📱
CapCut (Full Day Only)
Download the free CapCut desktop app before the session. No prior editing experience needed — we'll walk through everything from scratch. Full Day participants only.
🎯
One Project to Work With
Come prepared with one real project — photos, key features, and your target audience. You'll use this as your working brief during the session and leave with an actual video for it.
🔑
ChatGPT Account
A paid ChatGPT account is recommended. A free account works if you don't have the paid version, but some features may be limited.
📍
Venue & Zoom Details
Exact venue address (F2F) and Zoom link (online) will be provided to confirmed participants only, after payment has been received and verified.
