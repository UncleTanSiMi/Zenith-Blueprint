Why ESG Feels Hard

Sustainability matters. But it often gets stuck.

These are the three blockers we hear from Singapore SMEs every week.

🌀

Frameworks & Jargon Overwhelm

GRI, TCFD, ISSB, MAS guidelines, SGX requirements — the acronym soup is real. Without expert guidance, most teams don't know where to start.

🧩

Unclear Roles, Fragmented Efforts

ESG ownership is scattered. Marketing says one thing, operations another. Without a shared roadmap, efforts stay siloed and never add up to real progress.

📄

Reports That Don't Drive Action

Companies produce reports to tick boxes, but the data doesn't feed back into decision-making. Sustainability stays a comms exercise instead of a business strategy.

FAQ

Common Questions

Still figuring out if ESG consulting is right for your business right now? These are the questions we hear most often.

Ask Us Directly →
We're a small SME — is ESG consulting even relevant for us? +
Yes — and increasingly so. Singapore's regulatory environment is tightening: SGX-listed companies already have mandatory sustainability reporting, and supply chain ESG requirements are flowing down to SME suppliers. Beyond compliance, ESG unlocks green grants, strengthens tender responses, and differentiates you with customers who are increasingly asking about sustainability credentials. Our Start tier is specifically designed for SMEs at the very beginning of this journey.
Do you offer one-off projects or only retainers? +
Both. We typically recommend retainer engagements for ongoing support — ESG is a continuous process, not a one-time report. But we also support companies on a project basis for specific initiatives: a materiality assessment, a standalone sustainability report, a grant application, or a Green Manager training programme. We'll recommend what genuinely fits your needs.
What reporting frameworks do you work with? +
We work with GRI (Global Reporting Initiative), TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures), ISSB standards, SGX sustainability reporting guidelines, and MAS requirements. We tailor the framework mix to your company type, listing status, industry, and the stakeholders you need to report to.
How long does a typical ESG engagement take? +
The Start package can be completed in 4–8 weeks. Shape typically runs 3–6 months depending on scope and your team's availability. Sustain is designed as an ongoing annual engagement with quarterly touchpoints. We build the timeline around your business calendar, not the other way around.
Can you help us access green grants? +
Yes. We're familiar with Enterprise Singapore's sustainability-related support schemes, BCA Green Mark pathways, and NEA programmes. As part of our Sustain engagement — and as a standalone add-on — we help you identify the right grants, prepare the documentation, and position your ESG work to maximise your chances of approval.
What makes Zenith Blueprint different from a pure sustainability firm? +
We combine ESG expertise with a business-first mindset. We understand SME constraints — budget, headcount, competing priorities — and we connect ESG directly to your sales positioning, stakeholder communications, and grant strategy. We also bring AI-powered efficiency tools to the data collection and reporting process, which reduces your team's workload significantly.
